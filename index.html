Guilherme de Melo

I am currently a Master of Science student at the Institute of Geophysics and Planetary Physics, Scripps Institution of Oceanography, UC San Diego. I am working with David Sandwell, Steven Constable, and Peter Shearer. My interest is to understand the effect of spreading rate and crustal age variation on the seismic wave propagation of the uppermost mantle and lithosphere in different ocean basins.

I was visiting research at the Institut Universitaire Européen de la Mer, Université de Bretagne Occidentale, in 2018, Scripps Institution of Oceanography, UC San diego, in 2019, and at the Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory, Columbia University (2020).
